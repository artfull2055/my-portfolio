Who I Am

I’m someone who enjoys staying active, learning, and constantly growing. My interests range from high-energy sports to the structure and discipline found in martial arts. I’m also passionate about music, creativity, and the world of criminal justice — a field that has always intrigued me for its complexity and real-world impact.

What Drives Me

I value hard work, self-improvement, and dedication. Whether it’s training, studying, or learning new skills, I enjoy pushing myself to become better than I was yesterday.

Interests & Passions

  • Boxing and martial arts for discipline and strength
  • Sports of all kinds, especially those that require skill and strategy
  • Music, production, and discovering new artists
  • Criminal justice systems and how they shape society

My Values

Respect, discipline, honesty, and consistency guide the way I work and interact with people. I’m committed to learning, improving, and building a future driven by discipline and ambition.
